Feature Phone : Message App

CHOOSE_RECEIVER

This is a feature phone emulator with an SMS app. As such, the screen is not (clickable/touchable). The Left & Right selection buttons have to be used to select the respective actions in the screen footer.

While writing a new message, click you can switch from uppercase/lowercase/number mode using the * key.

Example: To type "Hello"

  1. Press * once to switch to uppercase
  2. Press 4 twice
  3. Press * twice to switch to lowercase
  4. Press 3 twice
  5. Press 5 thrice.
  6. Wait ~ 1.5 seconds
  7. Press 5 thrice.
  8. Press 6 thrice.
